I am an operations and product manager based in New York. My father wanted me to become a doctor. I chose startups instead, and have been part of them since I was 21. What drew me in then still drives me now: building things from scratch and figuring out hard, ambiguous problems without a playbook.

Most of my career has been spent in spirits and beverage. I have always loved the industry because it is built around real interactions in real life. Over the years, I have built and run distilleries across Europe and Asia, and the products I have created have won more than 30 industry awards across 40 productions. But the awards were never the point. What I love is building something real, then seeing it out in the world.

When problems need solving, I go first to the people closest to the product. Half a day in a distillery with the team will usually tell you more than a spreadsheet ever will. I am systems-minded enough to build the database and people-first enough to know that it is never the whole story.

I currently consult across the Americas and Asia, embedding on site when the work requires it.
