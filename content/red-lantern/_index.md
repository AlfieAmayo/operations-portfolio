Red Lantern Dining is a 14-site food and beverage franchise across Southeast Asia. This section documents the RTD beverage programme I designed and delivered: a centralised bottled drinks operation built from scratch across a seven-node supply chain. The centrepiece is a live dashboard reconstructing order history, COGS trajectory, and supplier reliability across two drinks and 18 production orders.
