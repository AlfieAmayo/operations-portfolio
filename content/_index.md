This portfolio documents operational work across two contexts: a craft distillery I co-own and operate in Phnom Penh, Cambodia, and an RTD beverage programme I designed and ran for a franchise dining group. Each piece focuses on a specific failure, a gap in visibility or detection, and what I built to address it. The work is real. Where names are fictionalised, the operational data is not.

The Seekers Spirits section covers two production incidents investigated as formal postmortems: a botanical contamination that propagated undetected across four batches, and a cooling system failure that threatened a time-critical export shipment. Both are backed by a PostgreSQL database built retrospectively from production records.

The Red Lantern Dining section covers the supply chain visibility programme I built for a 14-site franchise rollout. The centrepiece is a live dashboard reconstructing order history, COGS trajectory, and supplier reliability across two drinks and 18 production orders.
