<h1 class="page-title">Cooling System Failure</h1>

<p class="home-statement-primary">Temperature was visible during production, but it was neither recorded nor tied to an alert.</p>

<p class="body-p">On 29 May 2023, Batch 50, a Seekers Mekong Dry Gin run on the 2000L still, failed post-production quality control. The failure was detected 14 days later. By then, botanicals for a second run had already been staged, but preparation was halted, limiting the blast radius to one batch.</p>
